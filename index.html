About Me

Hi, I'm Krisy Chris, a graphic designer and UI/UX designer based in Sunyani Ghana. I have a passion for creating visually stunning designs that also function seamlessly for users. With three(3) years of experience in the industry, I have worked on projects ranging from branding and marketing materials to websites and mobile apps. My design philosophy is to always put the user first and ensure that every element of a design serves a purpose. When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring new creative hobbies and seeking out inspiration from the world around me.
